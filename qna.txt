Isay technical zuban mein **"Intranet Application"** ya **"Internal Tool"** kehte hain.

Iska farq presentation mein samjhane ke liye ye points use karein:

1. **Public Website (.com) Nahi Hai:**
Yeh Facebook ya Daraz jaisi website nahi hai ke koi bhi banda `www.intellibank.com` likh kar khol le. Yeh Aam Public (Customers) ke liye nahi hai.
2. **Private Network (Bank Servers):**
SRS mein likha hai ke ye system **"On-Premises"** (Bank ki building ke andar walay servers) ya **"Private Cloud"** par chalega.


* Sirf **Bank Managers** aur **Analysts** isay access kar sakenge.


* Unhein access karne ke liye **Secure Login** aur **Bank ka VPN** chahiye hoga.




3. **Analogy (Misaal) Presentation ke liye:**
"Sir, jaise Restaurant mein **'Dining Area'** sab ke liye hota hai lekin **'Kitchen'** mein sirf staff ja sakta hai.
* Bank ki **Mobile App** 'Dining Area' hai (Customers ke liye).
* Humara **IntelliBank** 'Kitchen' hai (Sirf Bank Managers ke liye taake wo business control kar sakein)."



**Conclusion:**
Yeh internet par publicly open nahi hogi, balki Bank ke apne secure internal network par chalegi taake data leak na ho.
